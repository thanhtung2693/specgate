# Supplier Listing Refresh PRD (excerpt)

## Problem
Buyers cannot filter supplier results by region + certification together.

## Goals
- Combined region + certification filter.
- Sort by last-updated certification date.

## Success metrics
- Median time to find a compliant supplier drops below 30 seconds.
- Filter adoption above 25 percent of supplier-detail navigations.

## Out of scope
- Editing supplier records inline.
- Cross-region duplicate detection.
